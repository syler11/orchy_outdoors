About Us

Located on the West Highland Way at Bridge of Orchy our Eco Pods were designed and built in Finland in 2016. They each sleep a maximum of 2 adults and have views to the river Orchy and Ben Dorain. The pods are featuring a wet room with toilet and shower is 3 min walk from the Pods.Food can be ordered at The Bridge of Orchy Hotel, which is a 5 minute walk away.
